These vessels explore form through computational strategies built into Grasshopper, a plugin for Rhino3D. Initially each vessel is 3D printed with PLA plastic which is then molded into a multi-part cast for ceramic slip casting. There is an objective to digitally generate forms that would be difficult for an artisan to produce with hand-made methods but would still rely on traditional techniques (slip casting) to reproduce each piece.

Almost in a lovecraftian fashion, the surfaces of these vessel temporarily erupt from the traditional forms that once grounded them to the real world. In doing so, each vessel constantly dances around this tension between artistic crafts and mechanical production, organic and artficial.
