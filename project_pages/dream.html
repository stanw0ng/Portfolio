Landing Page

This project integrates two opposing states, the organic and the artificial, to create a unique digital experience that challenges traditional ideas of social media. Moreover, it challenges the idea that there is nothing more boring that hearing someone talk about their dreams

Project Prompt

On this platform, dreams are the primary "content" for users to interact with and manipulate. Users catalog there dreams through typed text, sound files or imagery for interpretion. Once the dream is submitted into a cloud of other dreams, other users can choose to interpret; however, in a medium different to the the original submission. For example, a voice recording can only be decoded through imagery or text. This allows for a gradual process where dreams are transformed into completely different abstractions of the mind. Echoing the game of exquisite corpse invented by the Surrealists, this project celebrates the chaotic nature of interpretation as a highly creative process.
