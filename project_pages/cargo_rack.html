CARGO RACK (2020)

Materials:
Cold Rolled Steel

Client:
Personal

A steel cargo rack formed by a series of bends on a Hossfeld Bender and TIG-welded together. The metal is left to rust for a month and then sealed with a clear finish that captures the patina. An updated version has an angled back to better support a Wald basket.
