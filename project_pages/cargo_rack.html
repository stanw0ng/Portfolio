A TIG welded porteur rack primarily made of 3/16" and 1/16" cold rolled steel rods. The spider web motif was inspired by custom motorcycle and messenger culture and provides more points for a cargo net to latch onto. The metal is initially left to rust, creating a striking patina. It is finally finished with a clear coat that weatherproofs the metal and halts further rusting.
