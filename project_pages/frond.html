This piece considers the integration of CNC milling with traditional woodworking and joinery techniques. The table top celebrates the industrial qualities of birch plywood as it gently rests ontop of a half lapped frame made of solid hard maple. The legs of the table are calibrated to match the openings of the table top, establishing a delicate balance between the primary elements.
